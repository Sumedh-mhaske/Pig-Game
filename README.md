<h1>Pig game</h1>

Pig Game is a fun and addictive dice game where players take turns rolling a
single die to accumulate points. The objective of the game is to be the
first player to reach 100 points.

<h3>Rules</h3>

1. The game is played between two players, taking turns.<br>
2. On each turn, a
player rolls a single die as many times as they wish, accumulating points
for each roll.<br>
3. The player's turn ends when they decide to "hold" (stop
rolling) or if they roll a 1.<br>
4. If a player rolls a 1, their turn ends, and
they lose all points accumulated during that turn.<br>
5. The first player to
reach the predetermined target score (e.g., 100 points) wins the game.<br>

<h3>Getting Started</h3>

To play the Pig Game, simply clone or download the repository to your local
machine. No additional setup or installation is required.<br>

<h3>How to Play</h3>

1. Open the `index.html` file in your preferred web browser.<br>
2. The game will start automatically, and you'll see the game board with two players
displayed.<br>
3. Click the "Roll Dice" button to roll the die and accumulate
points for your current turn.<br>
4. If you're satisfied with your current
score, click the "Hold" button to add your points to your total score and
switch turns to the other player.<br>
5. If you roll a 1, your current turn
score will be reset to 0, and the turn will switch to the other player.<br>
6. The first player to reach the target score (100 points by default) wins the
game.<br>
